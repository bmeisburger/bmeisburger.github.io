    Creamy Sun-dried Tomato Chicken Pasta

    June 2, 2022 · 45 min

    Ingredients:#

    • 1 (8 oz) jar of sun-dried tomatoes
    • 1-1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, cubed
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • Red pepper flakes
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
    • 2 tablespoons salted butter
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 12 oz short cut pasta (rigatoni)
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
    • 1 cup fresh Thai basil
    • Zest of one lemon

    Instructions:#

    1. Drain 3 tablespoons of oil from the sun-dried tomato jar into a large pot or skillet with sides. Chop the sun-dried tomatoes and set aside.
    2. Set a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken, 1 teaspoons each of basil, oregano, and thyme, the paprika, and a pinch each of red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes. Add 1/4 cup parmesan, cook another minute, then remove the chicken from the pot.
    3. To the same pan, add the butter, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon basil, and 1/2 teaspoon oregano. Cook until fragrant, about 3 minutes. Add 3 1/2 cups water. Bring to a boil, add the pasta, and cook, stirring often, until the pasta is al dente, about 8 minutes. Stir in the cream, mustard, parmesan, Thai basil, and chopped sun-dried tomatoes. Slide the chicken and any juices left on the plate.
    4. Serve the pasta topped with lemon zest and fresh parmesan.
