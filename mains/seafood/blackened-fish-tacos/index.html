    Blackened Fish Tacos

    June 2, 2022 · 5 min

    Ingredients:#

    Fish and Assembly:#

    • 2 lbs Mahi-Mahi, cod, or halibut, cut into 2 inch pieces
    • Neutral oil for searing
    • Mango salsa or pico de gallo
    • Flour tortillas

    Spice Rub:#

    • 1 tablespoon chili powder
    • 1 tablespoon paprika
    • 1 tablespoon cumin
    • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon oregano
    • 1 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

    Avocado Cilantro Sauce#

    • 7 oz Greek yogurt
    • 1 cup fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1/2 avocado
    • 2 limes, zested and juiced
    • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1/3 cup water, plus more as needed
    • 1 jalapeño
    • 1 teaspoon chili oil, or to taste

    Instructions:#

    1. Blend together all ingredients for avocado cilantro sauce, adding more water as needed for viscous but pourable consistency.
    2. Evenly coat all sides of fish pieces in spice rub.
    3. Add oil to sauté pan over medium-high heat. Sear fish in batches until crisp and/or blackened, about 4 minutes per batch.
    4. Serve in tortillas with avocado sauce and mango pico de gallo.
