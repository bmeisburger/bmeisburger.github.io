    Tinto de Verano

    June 2, 2022 · 5 min

    Ingredients:#

    • 1 bottle of young and fruity red wine
    • Equal amount of lemon-flavored soda (or substitute with equal mixture of lemonade and sparkling water)
    • 1 orange, sliced
    • 1 lemon, sliced
    • 6 oz sweet vermouth (optional)
    • Ice

    Instructions:#

    1. Pour the wine into a large pitcher.
    2. Add the soda and stir slightly.
    3. Add the lemon and orange slices, as well as the vermouth if using. Stir just to combine.
    4. Serve over ice.
