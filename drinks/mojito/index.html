    Classic Mojito

    June 1, 2022 · 5 min

    Ingredients:#

    • 1 lime, quartered
    • 3/4 oz granulated white sugar or 1 oz simple syrup
    • 2 oz white rum
    • 5-8 mint leaves
    • Crushed ice
    • 4-6oz sparkling water, preferably Topo Chico

    Instructions:#

    1. Add quartered lime, sugar, and mint to a Tom Collins glass and muddle for 30 seconds.
    2. Fill the remainder of the glass with crushed ice.
    3. Pour the rum over the ice.
    4. Fill with sparkling water to the rim.
    5. Stir briefly.
    6. Garnish with mint sprig.
